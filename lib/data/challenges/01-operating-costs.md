# Challenge 1: High Operating Costs & Inflation

## Problem Sections

Rising energy, supply chain, and wage costs are creating severe margin pressure for Central Queensland SMEs. Businesses that operated profitably at 20% margins are now struggling with 8-12% margins or thin/negative profitability.

This is particularly acute for Hospitality & Retail (energy costs up 30-45% in past 24 months, food and beverage COGS up 15-25%, labour increases 8-12% annually), Manufacturing & Distribution (fuel surcharges, electricity for production facilities, raw material costs), and Healthcare & Professional Services (rent, utilities, staff wages especially difficult in regional areas).
