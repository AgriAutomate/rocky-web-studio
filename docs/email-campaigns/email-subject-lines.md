Quick reference for email subject lines and preview text.

## Subject Line Best Practices

### Length
- **Optimal:** 30-50 characters
- **Mobile:** Keep under 40 characters
- **Desktop:** Can go up to 60 characters

### Personalization
- Use first name when possible
- Reference their industry
- Mention specific benefits

### Avoid
- ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation (!!!)
- Spam trigger words
- Misleading claims

---

## Preview Text Tips

### Length
- **Optimal:** 40-130 characters
- **Mobile:** 40-90 characters visible
- **Desktop:** Up to 130 characters

### Purpose
- Expand on subject line
- Create urgency
- Highlight benefit
- Address objection

### Examples
- `Get your custom proposal in 24 hours`
- `Just 5 minutes - no obligation`
- `See what we built for similar businesses`
- `Industry insights you'll find valuable`

## Testing Recommendations

### Test These Variations

**Email 1:**
- Personalization: With/without first name
- Benefit: "Custom Proposal" vs. "Website Solution"
- Urgency: "24 hours" vs. "Quick"

**Email 2:**
- Time: "2 minutes" vs. "Quick"
- Question: Direct question vs. statement
- Reminder: "Reminder" vs. "Quick Question"

**Email 3:**
- Industry: Specific vs. generic
- Value: "Trends" vs. "Insights"
- Personal: "I Learned" vs. "We Found"

## Performance Tracking

Track these metrics:
- Open rate by subject line
- Click rate by preview text
- Conversion rate by variation
- Unsubscribe rate

Optimize based on:
- Highest open rates
- Highest click rates
- Lowest unsubscribe rates
- Highest form submissions
